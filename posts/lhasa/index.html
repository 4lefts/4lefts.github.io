Lhasa

By Stephen Ball, 04 June 2017

I remembered I own a couple of fm3 buddha machines.

It’s surprising how much freedom there is in a few well-made loops, a pitch and a volume control (and the original buddha machine didn’t even let you control pitch).

Anyway, this experiment is me trying to make something similar in the browser. It will probably be nothing like it. Plus the loop I’m using at the moment is not well made. I need to make some loops. A work in progress.

As always, built with p5 js, and Tone js. As a side note, this app is my first go at using Webpack rather than Gulp to bundle/transpile javascript.

Stephen Ball - 2017
